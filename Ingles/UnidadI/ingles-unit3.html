Unidad 3 – Present Perfect & Past Simple

El Past Simple se usa con tiempos definidos como yesterday o last year. El Present Perfect se emplea para experiencias, acciones sin tiempo concreto y resultados que afectan al presente.

Ejercicios interactivos

I ___ (see) that movie three times.

She ___ (not finish) her homework yet.

We ___ (go) to the park yesterday.

They ___ (live) in Madrid since 2019.

My parents ___ (just arrive) home.
